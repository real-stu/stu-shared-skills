# Worked Examples — @girls Story Series

Two full, real, high-performing series to calibrate tone, specificity, and structure against
before writing. Read these when drafting a new series.

## Example: Sev Laser — Glow-Up Edition (246K engagements)

Game format: This or That
Edition: Glow-Up Edition

**Slide 1 Hook:**
> LET'S PLAY — This or That: Glow-Up Edition ✨ — Pick Your Preference — Presented by @sevlaseraesthetics

**Slides 2-6 polls:**
- "Snatched & Smooth" → Eye Cream (90%) vs Botox (10%)
- "Pout Perfection" → Overline Lip (78%) vs Lip Filler (22%)
- "Cheekbones on cloud nine" → Cheek Filler (10%) vs Contour (90%)
- "Dew It Up" → Chug Water (81%) vs SKINVIVE (19%)
- "Bye Bye, Jaw Jiggle" → Kybella (17%) vs Gua Sha (83%)

**Slide 7 CTA:**
> Glow-up with a FREE Botox/Filler consultation! — FIND A SEV NEAR YOU 📍 — Available in select locations

*Note: This series ran 7 published slides instead of 10. The 10-slide version is now the default for all future deployments — the second Sev Laser series at 10 slides still drove 232K and gave more inventory to sell against.*

## Example: Picsart — Editing Pics Edition 2.0 (148K engagements)

Game format: Red Flag or Green Flag
Edition: Editing Pics Edition 2.0

**Slide 1 Hook:**
> Picsart — Red Flag or Green Flag — Editing Pics Edition 2.0 — Presented by @picsart

**Slides 2-9 polls (behaviors, not products):**
- "They make AI versions of your future kids" → Red flag 47% / Green flag 53%
- "They remove photobombers in the background" → Red 25% / Green 75%
- "They watermark every single edit they make" → Red 62% / Green 38%
- "They send you 17 edited versions of the same photo" → Red 58% / Green 42%
- "They edit themselves into your baby photo" → Red 66% / Green 34%
- "They edited you into a Vogue cover for your birthday" → Red 17% / Green 83%
- "They replaced your ex with a houseplant" → Red 28% / Green 72%
- "They added themselves into your family Christmas card" → Red 56% / Green 44%

**Slide 10 CTA:**
> Posting unedited photos 🚩 — Editing with Picsart ✅ — TAP TO DOWNLOAD

*Note: No explicit soft-sell slot here — the final slide carries all the conversion weight. This is a valid variant when the game itself (Red Flag/Green Flag) is inherently positioning the product.*
