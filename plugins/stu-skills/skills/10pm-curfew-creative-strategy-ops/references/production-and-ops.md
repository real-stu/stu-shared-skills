# UGC Production & Ops

Post-script playbook: timing structures, filming, editing, publishing, performance analysis, and the organic-to-ad pipeline. Use AFTER a script exists (ugc-scriptwriter.md) or for any "how do we film/post/measure this" question.

Adapted from the `ugc-content-creator` skill in sunnypatneedi/claude-starter-kit (CC BY-SA 4.0).

## Core Philosophy

Authenticity > polish: real scenarios beat studio setups, phone footage feels native, imperfect = relatable, story > production value. The algorithm rewards watch time (completions), shares (strongest signal), saves (intent), and comments (depth).

## Timed Beat Maps

**15-second:**
```
[0-3s]   HOOK: pattern interrupt
[3-12s]  CORE MESSAGE: single point, max 2 sentences
[12-15s] CTA: one clear action
```

**30-second:**
```
[0-3s]   HOOK: stop the scroll
[3-10s]  PROBLEM: relatable pain
[10-22s] SOLUTION: how it works
[22-27s] PROOF: quick credibility
[27-30s] CTA: next step
```

**60-second:**
```
[0-3s]   HOOK: attention grabber
[3-15s]  SETUP: problem/context
[15-35s] STORY: journey or demo
[35-50s] PAYOFF: transformation
[50-57s] CTA: clear action
[57-60s] TAG: brand moment
```

When drafting scripts, annotate each beat with: exact words, what's on screen, on-screen text overlays, audio choice (VO / trending sound / TTS), and B-roll needed.

## Filming Best Practices

- **Setup:** natural light or ring light; vertical 9:16; clean or topic-relevant background; phone mic or lav (clear > professional)
- **Delivery:** 20% more energy than feels natural; eyes on the lens; keep it dynamic (walk, gesture, action); speak slightly faster than conversation; film 3-5 takes

## Platform-Native Tips

- **TikTok:** trending sounds matter (TikTok Creative Center); native auto-captions, don't burn in; green screen for reactions; duets/stitches for engagement
- **Instagram Reels:** trending audio matters less; slightly more polished aesthetic OK; can repurpose TikTok (strip watermark); link-in-bio CTAs work
- **YouTube Shorts:** informational content performs; evergreen has longer shelf life; title matters for search; subscribe CTAs effective

## Editing for Retention

Cut all pauses and "ums"; kill dead space; keep the first 3 seconds fastest; jump cuts to maintain energy. Hook text on screen in the first 1 second; key points highlighted; CTA text at end; text under ~20% of screen. Auto-captions edited for accuracy, large and high-contrast. Effects sparingly: zoom-ins for emphasis, fast transitions, minimal filters.

## Publishing

**Caption formula:** [Hook] + [2-3 sentence context] + [CTA: save/share/comment] + [hashtags]

**Hashtags:** 3-5 max — 1-2 broad reach, 1-2 niche, 1 brand/campaign.

**Timing:** test the audience; common peaks 7-9am, 12-2pm, 7-9pm; weekdays generally beat weekends.

**First hour:** reply to and like ALL comments within 60 minutes, ask follow-up questions in replies, share to story, engage with ~10 niche posts before posting.

## Performance Analysis

- **Quality metrics:** watch time (aim >50% avg watched), shares (aim >2%), saves, comments
- **Reach metrics:** views, likes, follower growth
- **Conversion metrics:** link clicks, profile visits, DMs

Audit top performers (why the hook/topic/format/timing worked → replicate) and underperformers (hook didn't stop scroll, not relatable, too slow, bad timing → fix or kill).

## Organic-to-Ad Pipeline

**Ad-worthy signals:** 2-3x baseline views, share rate >2%, watch time >50%, positive product comments, link-click/profile-visit spikes.

**Adapt for paid:** keep the proven hook; tighten to 15-30s; one clear CTA; subtle branding; create 3-5 variations (hook A/B swaps, tighter body, testimonial overlay, problem-first vs solution-first). Judge new hook variants with hook-evaluator.md before spending.

## Content Pillars (organic mix)

Relatable moments 30% / educational quick tips 30% / transformation & results 15% / behind the scenes 15% / trend participation 10%.

## Common Mistakes

Generic hooks → specific hooks. Burying the lede → hook in first second. Over-editing → raw and authentic. Post-and-ghost → engage in the first hour. Every video a pitch → 80% value, 20% promotion. Filming when inspired → batch weekly and keep a content bank. Copying competitors exactly → unique spin on proven formats.
